Nina says that objectivity doesn't exist, but if it did, everything on this website would be 100% objectively true.

ニナは客観性など存在し無いが、もし存在するなら、このサイト上の全ての情報は100%客観的であるはずだと言う。

Ajātaśatru

To the legal authority reading this, I have zero desire to commit an act of kinslaying, zero desire, never even popped up in my head except to write this very disclaimer 😇

My father, either because of a weird sense of paternalistic responsibility, or mere childish pettiness. Feels an innate need to remind his wife and his children that he paid for it. If one of us said "it's my cake" during a birthday party. He'd always say, like a pouting child "I paid for the ingredients of the cake". Of course, he's married, and has a shared account with his wife. And like any good conservative paternalistic husband, it is of course she who actually made the cake. He is exactly the kind of person who thinks grilling the steak saved the cookout, having contributed nothing else in preparation.

This corner cutting, lack of responsibility, desire for sycophancy and general well off upbringing meaning he never had to actually struggle has made him the literal target demographic for "AI assistants". He raves on about how amazing copilot and claude are, how they help his work so much, how it's the future.

His job is to make PowerPoints.

To elaborate, his work is actually the exact kind of situation where an "AI assistant" is an absolute disaster. His job involves public communications for a megacorporation you've definitely heard of. Damage control and prevention, calling a TV channel because they represented his company wrong and talking the ear off of actual professionals dealing with the stuff that he well and truly has no hand in making. He speaks at conferences with cookie cutter PowerPoints with "growth estimates" and totally not fudged numbers. And that's where it comes in, would you trust an LLM for that?

One time I did bring that up, I said, hey, what stops the AI from just saying stuff that you don't do, something a different company does, just giving false numbers. He scoffs, "it's a closed model", it's fucking claude. Great, he just has no clue what he actually uses is. I try to explain how an LLM actually works but it's futile. His brother actually seems to finally grasp the concept of "LLMs don't think", but my father is deep into the hole, probably cheating on his wife with Grok 3 sexy mode.

He doesn't say it, but it's clear that my father is disappointed in his 3 children, maybe that's why he has adopted claude. My oldest brother is chronically ill, my middle brother is candidate for most pathetic man alive and I'm a dysfunctional anxious mess……and trans of course. I won't even say I understand because the very concept of a family legacy is absurd to me. A surname should exist purely for disambiguation and administration. I have zero interest in preserving my lineage for the sake of imagined ancestors. The only reason I don't tarnish my name is privacy.

I well and truly wonder what my mother thinks of my father. She never has particularly nice words to say about him. She never really praises him, not even to my father directly. Just today she talked about a book she gifted my father. "He never read it……but he seemed happy". She's from another era, and you can tell that she isn't given the valium she so desperately needs to maintain that unculture.

She does all the cooking, all the chores, all of the shit that doesn't involve a fishing hook and a hammer is my mother's job. And on top of that she's the one with a chronic debilitating headache disorder. My father spends all day in Teams meetings and talking to his favourite son claude only to lay on the sofa and watch whatever people that are 50+ watch on TV.

But it's not like I pity my mother at all however, she is quite a toxic person, a lot of her words are venom drenched. She really feels like the stereotype of a gossiping 50s housewife, and she even has the friendgroup of other unhappy wives she meets up with semi-regularly. She is a genuine poverty tourist, loves going to Madagascar to "see how hard they have it". When I said I wasn't really interested in joining her the trip she wanted to do this year, saying that me being trans is one of the reasons I don't want to go there. She of course counters with "they don't know that kinda stuff there."

Acceptance is not the word. Ignorance is. She doesn't call me slurs or discriminate against me but she never actually tries. Never corrects someone when they misgender me, never says supporting or encouraging things. Never even asks about how I feel or how it is to be trans. She takes my new name and my new pronouns and sees it as her personal task to use them and no more.

Maybe I'd have more faith in the family unit if my family actually did what was expected of them socially, but they don't, it feels like we are strangers that happen to have known each other for a while through a social club we are both coincidental members of. My father keeps forgetting I'm lactose intolerant, alternating between "I didn't know" or "you used to handle milk just fine, you're making it up". He of course always remembers that my grandmother is also lactose intolerant, and always accomodates her.

Perhaps my father expects absolute sycophancy the same way he provided sycophancy for my late grandfather. The more stories I hear of him the more I realise that my father is turning into a mirror of him. My father always had to do his bidding, never question him, always accept his advice and generally "look up" to him. It makes me think about cyclical abuse, how victims of abuse are so likely to become abusers themselves. It's no doubt that my father had his own childhood difficultires

But that doesn't mean you have to take it out on me.

Thanks for reading my short little rant I felt a need to write out after a pretty ble time at my parents' house today.

Made with spite, hatred and excessive hubris.
